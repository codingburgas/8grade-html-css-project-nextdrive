Privacy Policy

At NextDrive, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

We do not collect any personal data from visitors unless voluntarily provided. Any data provided by users will be used exclusively for the purpose of providing services on our website.

2. Cookies

This website does not use any cookies or tracking technologies to collect personal data. Our website may, however, use cookies for essential functionalities, such as keeping you logged in or remembering your language preferences.

We do not share any of your personal data with third-party services unless required by law. No third-party tracking or advertising services are used on our site.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@nextdrive.com.
