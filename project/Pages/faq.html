Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to rent a car?

Yes, a valid credit card is required for all bookings and deposits.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum rental age is 21 years old. Drivers under 25 may pay a young driver surcharge.

Can I pick up and return a car in different cities?

Yes, NextDrive offers one-way rentals between selected locations. Extra fees may apply.
